About Me

Early Life & Education

Born and raised in Nagaland, I completed my education with a strong belief that knowledge is the foundation of progress. My journey began with a vision to serve my people and bring positive change to our society.

Political Journey

As a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party, I have dedicated my career to public service. Representing my constituency with pride, I work tirelessly to address the needs of my people and promote the rich cultural heritage of Nagaland.

Community Leadership

My role as Minister of Higher Education and Tourism has allowed me to champion educational initiatives and promote Nagaland's unique cultural identity. I believe in leading by example and fostering unity through our shared heritage.

Vision & Values

Education First

Empowering our youth through quality education and skill development to build a brighter future for Nagaland.

Unity Through Culture

Preserving and promoting our rich Naga heritage while fostering unity and understanding among diverse communities.

Digital Empowerment

Bridging the digital divide and leveraging technology to create opportunities for growth and innovation in our region.

Transparent Governance

Committed to integrity, accountability, and transparency in public service to build trust and deliver effective governance.

Minister of Higher Education and Tourism

Launch of Tribal Affairs Portal

Spearheaded the launch of a comprehensive portal to streamline tribal affairs and improve access to government services for tribal communities.

2019

Release of ₹1274.01 Lakhs Scholarship Funds

Facilitated the release of substantial scholarship funds to support the education of deserving students from tribal communities.
